## About me

I'm currently a writer-of-all-words at [Fern](https://buildwithfern.com/) (a [Postman](http://postman.com/) company). I write developer documentation and technical marketing content (case studies, engineering blog posts, product launches, social media, etc) for Fern's Docs and SDKs. My [portfolio](/portfolio/) contains a selection of my work; my [GitHub profile](https://github.com/devalog) is a better indicator of how much I write.

Previously, I was a technical writer at Google for over 6 years. Before that, I was a technical writer intern at [MapR Technologies](http://community.hpe.com/t5/ai-unlocked/where-is-mapr-today/ba-p/7091891) (acquired by HP). I graduated from the College of William and Mary with a double major in English and Accounting and a minor in Computer Science. Here's my [resume](https://github.com/devalog/resume/blob/main/resume.pdf).

In my free time, I [read a lot](/2025/08/19/booklist.html). The best book I've read so far in 2026 is *The English Understand Wool* (2022) by Helen DeWitt, followed closely by *The Romance of a Shop* (1888) by Amy Levy.
